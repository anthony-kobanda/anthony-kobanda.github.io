About Me

I’m a researcher driven by curiosity and a passion for building intelligent systems that learn over time. Currently, I’m pursuing a PhD in Deep Reinforcement Learning at Inria Scool & Ubisoft (University of Lille), under the supervision of Odalric‑Ambrym Maillard, Rémy Portelas and Ludovic Denoyer.

My research focuses on continual reinforcement learning—developing agents that can adapt to new tasks without forgetting what they’ve already learned. I’m particularly interested in tackling challenges such as catastrophic forgetting, generalisation across domains and scalability. My long‑term goal is to design adaptive, efficient systems that can operate in diverse, real‑world environments.
